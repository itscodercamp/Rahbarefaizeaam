About Rahbar e Faiz e Aam Welfare Trust

Welcome to Rahbar e Faiz e Aam Welfare Trust, a beacon of hope and compassion founded by the visionary Bilal Ahmad Quasmi. We stand as a testament to the unwavering commitment to humanity, striving to make a meaningful difference in the lives of individuals across India.

Founder's Message

Bilal Ahmad Quasmi, the driving force behind Rahbar e Faiz e Aam, has dedicated his life to the service of humanity. Our Impact


Hospitality

In the spirit of hospitality, Rahbar e Faiz e Aam provides shelter and sustenance to those in need. Our shelters are not just roofs over heads but symbols of hope, warmth, and resilience.

Healthcare

We understand the value of health and well-being. The trust extends a healing touch through medical camps, clinics, and support for critical treatments, ensuring that health becomes a right, not a privilege.

Education

Education is the cornerstone of progress. Rahbar e Faiz e Aam invests in the future by providing educational opportunities, scholarships, and resources, unlocking the door to a brighter tomorrow.

Clothing and Food

Basic necessities are the foundation of a dignified life. We distribute clothing and food to vulnerable communities, recognizing that these essentials pave the way for a more equitable society.

Marriages

Celebrating love and commitment, Rahbar e Faiz e Aam supports marriages by providing financial assistance and organizing community events. We believe in fostering bonds that create a strong, supportive social fabric.

Employment

Empowerment through employment is a key focus. The trust facilitates skill development programs and job placement initiatives, empowering individuals to build sustainable livelihoods.

Funerals

In moments of grief, Rahbar e Faiz e Aam stands as a pillar of support. We assist in funeral arrangements, alleviating the burden on families during difficult times.

Hajj and Umrah Tours

For those with a lifelong dream of undertaking the sacred journey of Hajj and Umrah, the trust makes it possible. We believe in making spiritual aspirations a reality.
